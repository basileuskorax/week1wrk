Description

It's macaroni with cheese on it. Read more here: Wikipedia

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • White Cheese
  • Yellow Cheese
  • Blue Cheese
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Bacon

Step1

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Drain Pasta

Step2

  1. Melt White Cheese with Yellow Cheese
  2. Grate Blue Cheese over the melted cheese

Step3

  1. Put cheese over pasta in a pan.
  2. Put Bread Crumbs on top.
  3. Bake for 10 minutes.
  4. Crumble Bacon over and enjoy!
